From the Park to Purpose

Gary and Liza Kalawaia built Kidz Dat Shr3d after watching idle energy at the local park turn into something better — their son Kingsten pulling peers onto boards and into a positive path.

Today Kingsten competes hard and mentors at KDS events, helping new riders land first tricks while the org raises gear, hosts sessions, and works toward a dedicated action-sports hub for Pahrump youth.
